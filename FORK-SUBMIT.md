# Fork-and-submit — M10 Integration (Team)

1. The designated team submitter (typically the Infra-Integration lead)
   clicks **Use this template** on `LevelUp-Applied-AI/m10-i10` to
   create the team's working repo.
2. Add the other Team Members as collaborators with Write access.
3. Each Team Member works on the role-named branch documented in
   `TEAM.md`:
   - `backend/api-endpoints`
   - `frontend/nextjs-pages`
   - `infra/docker-compose`
4. Open one internal PR per role branch into the team fork's `main`,
   reviewed by the teammate specified in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
5. When all three role branches are merged and `docker compose up -d`
   smoke passes locally for every Team Member, the team submitter
   pastes the team fork URL into TalentLMS → Module 10 → Integration Task.
6. Each Team Member separately submits the participation-confirmation
   TalentLMS unit naming their assigned role.
